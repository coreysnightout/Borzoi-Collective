About Borzoi Collective

Borzoi Collective is a team of best friends: Kevin Thrakulchavee, Victoria Hilton, Adrian Cadena, Sid Daof and Corey Redding. The goal of Borzoi is to share and distribute local artists in the surrounding area of wherever we live. We enjoy duplicating tapes for bands (easy hand outs at a low price!) and/or print our own graphics whether it be for Borzoi or for the artists. We love setting up events where we have live artist, bands and merch sales. In the end, it's about having fun and sharing some laughs with good people.
